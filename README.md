# Journal Node: Financial Analysis AI Toolkit (v1.0)
**Standardized Toolkit for Post Fiat Network Operators**

This repository contains five highly optimized, tested AI prompts designed to extract market alpha and non-obvious financial insights. These prompts are engineered for "Journal Node" operators to parse complex data into actionable strategic intelligence.

---

## 1. Macro Synthesis: The "Shadow Pivot" Detector
* **Task:** Analyzing Central Bank communication for subtle shifts in policy trajectory.
* **Target Model:** Claude 3.5 Sonnet (Optimized for nuance and lexical analysis).
* **Prompt:** > "Act as a Quantitative Fed Watcher. Analyze the attached Fed Minutes/Statement. Identify 'lexical shifts'—specific words or phrases that have changed since the last three meetings. Focus on the tension between 'Productive Capacity' and 'Labor Market Tightness.' Extract any mention of 'liquidity backstops' or 'extraordinary measures.' Summarize the 'Shadow Pivot' probability on a scale of 1-10, where 10 is an imminent unannounced rate cut."
* **Example Output:** * *Finding:* Shift from 'highly attentive' to 'monitoring' regarding inflation risks.
    * *Insight:* Signals a transition from active tightening to a data-dependent hold.
    * *Shadow Pivot Score:* 7/10.

## 2. 10-K Risk Extraction: The "Contagion" Filter
* **Task:** Identifying off-balance-sheet risks and hidden liabilities in SEC filings.
* **Target Model:** GPT-4o (Optimized for cross-referencing tables and footnotes).
* **Prompt:** > "Analyze the 'Management’s Discussion and Analysis' (MD&A) and 'Footnotes' of this 10-K. Ignore standard boilerplate risks. Find: 1) Variable interest entities (VIEs) with high leverage, 2) Off-balance-sheet commitments exceeding 5% of market cap, and 3) Sensitivity to specific currency fluctuations management minimizes. Synthesize these into a 3-bullet 'Contagion Risk' summary."
* **Example Output:** * *Risk:* $1.2B in undisclosed vendor financing agreements.
    * *Impact:* Potential 12% hit to liquidity if credit conditions tighten.

## 3. Sentiment Analysis: The "Management Confidence" Audit
* **Task:** Detecting verbal hedging and evasive maneuvers during earnings Q&A.
* **Target Model:** Claude 3.5 Sonnet.
* **Prompt:** > "Compare the CEO’s prepared remarks in this transcript against their responses in the Q&A session. Identify three 'Divergence Points' where the language became more hedging or repetitive (e.g., excessive use of 'look,' 'obviously,' or 'at this time'). Explain the likely underlying financial concern for each divergence point (e.g., margin compression, supply chain lag)."
* **Example Output:** * *Divergence:* CEO used 'obviously' 5x when asked about Q3 guidance.
    * *Interpretation:* High uncertainty regarding regional bank exposure.

---

## 4. Regime Prediction: The "Yield Curve" Synthesis
* **Task:** Determining investment regimes based on interest rate structures.
* **Target Model:** Perplexity (for real-time data) / ChatGPT (for logic).
* **Prompt:** > "Using the current 2s/10s and 3m/10s yield curve data, identify if the economy is in a 'Bear Steepening' or 'Bull Flattening' regime. Based on the Post Fiat framework (prioritizing hard assets and non-sovereign money), analyze how this specific curve shape historically impacts the correlation between Gold, Bitcoin, and the S&P 500."
* **Example Output:** * *Regime:* Bear Steepening.
    * *Strategy:* Increase allocation to non-correlated hard assets; reduce duration in sovereign bonds.

---

## 5. Efficiency Audit: The "Margin Compression" Warning
* **Task:** Predicting future profitability drops by analyzing inventory vs. revenue growth.
* **Target Model:** GPT-4o.
* **Prompt:** > "Calculate the 'Inventory-to-Sales' growth delta for this company over the last 4 quarters. If inventory growth outpaces revenue growth by >15%, identify the specific product lines mentioned in the MD&A that are accumulating. Predict the likelihood of an inventory write-down in the next 6 months and the estimated basis point impact on Net Margin."
* **Example Output:** * *Calculation:* Inventory +22% vs. Revenue +4%. 
    * *Warning:* High probability of a 150bps margin hit in Q3 due to liquidations.
